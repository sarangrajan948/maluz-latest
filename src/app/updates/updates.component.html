Malavika

"Master actress Malavika Mohanan took the internet by storm with her latest photo-shoot pictures shot at the exotic locales of Karjat. Flaunting her voluptuous figure in a green skirt and knotted blouse, the 27-year-old diva can be seen posing on the black rocks in the wilderness.
